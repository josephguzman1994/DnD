Kingdoms of Asterfall

The conflict is not light against darkness. It is memory against survival.

The Halo Crown

A radiant monarchy that presents itself as the guardian of order, civilization, and sacred law. Its critics claim that its light is blinding by design.

The Ashen Freeholds

A confederation of hard-won territories, exile houses, city-states, and border clans. Its defenders call it freedom. Its enemies call it organized resentment.

The Dead Observatory

An ancient astronomical fortress whose instruments no longer watch the stars. Both kingdoms claim historical right to it. Neither admits what they fear lies beneath it.
